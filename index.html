About Ragdoll Football

Ragdoll Football 2 Players is a fun and chaotic multiplayer sports game that combines physics-based gameplay with football excitement. Play with a friend and experience the hilarious moments of ragdoll physics!

  • 2-player local multiplayer
  • Physics-based gameplay
  • Multiple game modes
  • Fun and engaging matches

Game Features

  • Realistic physics engine
  • Local multiplayer support
  • Various football fields
  • Simple controls
  • Funny ragdoll animations
  • Competitive gameplay

How to Play

1. Player 1: Use WASD keys to move

2. Player 2: Use Arrow keys to move

3. Try to score goals against your opponent

4. Use the physics to your advantage

5. First to reach the target score wins!
